Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani ingredients include Basmati rice, chicken, yogurt, onions, ginger-garlic paste, and a blend of whole and ground spices like cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, cumin, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. You'll also need ghee or oil, saffron, and fresh herbs like mint and coriander for flavor and garnish.
